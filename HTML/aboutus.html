About KD Landscaping

KD Landscaping is a Hebburn based landscape gardening business. We offer a range of professional services including garden design, patios and driveways. We have a wide base of customers from small domestic projects up to large corporate spaces. Our business has flourished over the years and we put this down to our friendly and approachable service, we listen to our clients needs and help guide them to the right solution. We offer a quality service which is endorsed by our membership with the Association of Professional Landscapers. Our team of landscapers and garden experts offer over 60 years of hard earned experience between them. We work closely with clients and garden designers alike to help realise the clients dream for their own special outdoor space. If you have an upcoming project and would like our advice please feel free to contact us.
